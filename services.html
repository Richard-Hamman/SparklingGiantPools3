Our Services

Services

Maintenance

Looking after a pool, even a small one, is very hard work and many people do not have the time or energy to keep up with the job of keeping a pool sparkling and blue. We can assist with maintenance services to always keep your pool in tip top condition and ready for use.

We are affiliated with The Pool Team and Waterwell Projects which means we can supply with a large variety of pool care products including balancing kits, chlorine, filters, pipes, lighting and much more.

New Pool Installations

We specialize in building beautiful, affordable pools. Take advantage of over 15 years of experience in the pool care and construction industry, and the personal service you will receive from this owner managed business.

We are based in Croydon, Kempton Park and service areas such as Edenvale, Morningside, Sandton, Woodmead, Houghton, Hyde Park, Rosebank, Bedfordview, Primrose, Germiston and the greater Johannesburg area.

Renovations

Do you need assistance with renovation or repair of your pool? Look no further – we can help!
Whether you need to repair cracks, refreshed marblite, or a major project like increasing or decreasing the size of your pool.

Take advantage of the personalized service and extra care you will get from us and call us for a quote today.

Jacuzzis

The therapeutic effects of hydrotherapy is unmistakable and you can make use of these benefits in the comfort of your own home with the installation of a jacuzzi.
We can assist with the professional installation of a jacuzzi of your choice.

Already have one installed? Then rely on us for maintenance and repairs to ensure your jacuzzi is ready for use at all times.

pH Balancing

Take the guess work out of balancing the water in your pool and call us for assistance. We assist with complete balancing of all aspects including the alkalinity, calcium hardness, stabilizer and total dissolved solids in the water.

Maintaining this is imperative to reduce the chance of growing algea and will ensure your pool is always clear, sparkling and ready for use.

Water Features

Create a tranquil space where you can reflect and listen to the sound of running water, by installing a beautifully designed water feature.

Our expert can assist with new installation or repair, renovation and maintenance of any water feature, fish tank or koi pond.

We are a family run business that specialises in Swimming Pools and Construction. From general repairs and maintenance to new developments.
