Who are we?

We are a family run business that specialises in Swimming Pools and Construction. From general repairs and maintenance to new developments.
We pride ourselves in our fast and friendly service, and the expertise of our staff is unparalleled.

Sparkling Giant pools is a trusted service provider of the Houghton Hotel and Golf Estate.

We can supply you with various pool care and maintenance products and equipment such as water balancing products, pool pumps, pipes and accessories and much more. Call us on 072 355 3520 or 064 474 0206 for more information.
